Investigate Influenza virus with entropic metric measures

Evolution of the hemagglutinin segment of H1N1 Influenza Virus.

Cluster 30 (ex 28) is still the most populated. Clusters 33, 34, 35 and 36 could be new emerging strains.
The plot shows the temporal evolution of influenza virus H1N1 in the last years. Different virus strains are identifying by horizontal colored lines (each spot identifies the hemagglutinin segment of an isolate, isolates are full lenght and come from all over the world). Note that virus strains do not appear suddenly, early signals are present even one year before in some cases.
