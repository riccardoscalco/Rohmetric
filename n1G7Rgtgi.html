Investigate Influenza virus with entropic metric measures

Evolution of the hemagglutinin segment of H1N1 Influenza Virus.

The plot shows the temporal evolution of influenza virus H1N1 in the last three years. Different virus strains are identifying by horizontal colored lines (each spot identifies the hemagglutinin segment of an isolate, isolates are full lenght and come from all over the world). Note that virus strains do not appear suddenly, early signals are present even one year before in some cases.
According to the analysis, new strains emerged on the winter season 2012/13 (clusters 21-25).

Credits

The above analysis are based on the theory developed by RB, RS, MC and described in Rohlin Distance and the Evolution of Influenza A Virus: Weak Attractors and Precursors (International Patent N. PCT/IB2012/051250).

Data Source

Data from the GISAID database, only complete amino acid sequences from all over the world are analyzed.
